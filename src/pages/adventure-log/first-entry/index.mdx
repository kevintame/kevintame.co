Every good adventure deserves a record. This is mine.

I've been writing articles for a while now — long-form thoughts on leadership, design, and the games I love. But there's a whole other category of things I do and places I go that never quite fit the "article" format. A hike that wrecked me in the best way. A trip that changed how I think about something. A day that was just worth remembering.

That's what this log is for.

The format is loose by design. Some entries will be long. Some will be short. The only rule is that something happened and it was worth writing down.
